# Blue Ocean Strategy 

## Introduction
The **Blue Ocean Strategy** is a strategic framework that helps businesses create new market spaces (or "blue oceans") by focusing on differentiation and reducing competition. It aims to shift from competing in existing markets ("red oceans") to creating uncontested market spaces where the business has a competitive edge.
