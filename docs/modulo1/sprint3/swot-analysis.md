# SWOT Analysis

## Introduction
A **SWOT analysis** is a strategic planning framework used to evaluate the **Strengths**, **Weaknesses**, **Opportunities**, and **Threats** of a project or business. It helps identify internal and external factors that can impact the success of a solution, allowing for better strategic decision-making and improved business performance.

### **Purpose of a SWOT Analysis**
- **Strengths** ‚Äì Identify the internal advantages that give the project a competitive edge.  
- **Weaknesses** ‚Äì Highlight internal limitations or obstacles that could hinder progress.  
- **Opportunities** ‚Äì Detect external factors that the project can capitalize on to grow.  
- **Threats** ‚Äì Recognize external challenges that could negatively affect the project.
